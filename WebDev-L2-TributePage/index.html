Elon Musk

Visionary entrepreneur shaping the future of transportation and space exploration.

Biography

Elon Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa, in 1971. From an early age, he displayed a deep interest in technology and innovation.

After moving to North America, he pursued studies in physics and economics before entering the world of entrepreneurship.

He played key roles in the growth of online payments, electric vehicles, and private spaceflight, helping bring these industries into the mainstream.

His ambitious goals, from sustainable energy to interplanetary travel, continue to inspire discussions about humanity’s future.

Major Milestones

  1. 1999 – Co-founded X.com, which later became PayPal.
  2. 2002 – Founded SpaceX.
  3. 2004 – Joined Tesla Motors as an early investor and chairman.
  4. 2021 – Became one of the world’s wealthiest individuals.
